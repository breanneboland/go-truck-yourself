Go Truck Yourself came from a sunny Seattle afternoon in summer 2014. I was eating a fish waffle from BeanFish, which I had recently learned was a thing in our world. Perfect fish-shaped waffles, perfectly sized, and sold in my city - and I had no idea. Why was there not a central resource for this, ideally in app form? Why didn't I know about this? The name came to me immediately. Some things are meant to be.

App reviews for food truck trackers in Apple's app store were abysmal, so I didn't bother. Instead, I filed my idea in the back of my head, next to my growing interest in learning programming. There will come a time, I thought. Then I went and got another fish waffle.

So here we are. You can learn more about me and my education here.
